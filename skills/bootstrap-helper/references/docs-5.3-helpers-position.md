### Responsive Sticky Top Positioning with Bootstrap

Source: https://getbootstrap.com/docs/5.3/helpers/position

Provides responsive variations for the `.sticky-top` utility, allowing elements to stick to the top of the viewport on specified screen sizes and wider. This enhances adaptability across different devices.

```html
<div class="sticky-sm-top">Stick to the top on viewports sized SM (small) or wider</div>
<div class="sticky-md-top">Stick to the top on viewports sized MD (medium) or wider</div>
<div class="sticky-lg-top">Stick to the top on viewports sized LG (large) or wider</div>
<div class="sticky-xl-top">Stick to the top on viewports sized XL (extra-large) or wider</div>
<div class="sticky-xxl-top">Stick to the top on viewports sized XXL (extra-extra-large) or wider</div>

```
